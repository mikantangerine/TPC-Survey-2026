About the Survey

The 2026 TPC Survey explores the backgrounds, interests and philosophical views of members of The Philosophy Community. Participants answered questions on demographics, philosophy, religion, education, politics, artificial intelligence and related topics.

This website presents the aggregated results through interactive charts generated directly from the survey responses.
